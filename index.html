About

Optimization for intelligent Earth observation mission planning.

I am a PhD student at the State Key Laboratory of Information Engineering in Surveying, Mapping and Remote Sensing (LIESMARS), Wuhan University.

My research explores how optimization models and learning-based methods can support dynamic Earth observation mission scheduling, SAR regional mapping, and spatio-temporal mobility analysis.

Research

Research Interests

01

Satellite Mission Scheduling

Dynamic task assignment, imaging window selection, and multi-point observation planning for agile Earth observation satellites.

02

Learning-Based Optimization

Meta reinforcement learning, proximal policy optimization, and spiking neural networks for complex scheduling decisions.

03

SAR Regional Mapping

Multi-strip stitching, energy-aware imaging plans, and radiometric consistency correction for SAR intensity images.

04

Spatio-Temporal Mobility

Taxi trajectory analysis, ride-hailing collaboration areas, and interday stability of urban travel flows.
